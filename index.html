Pes Patron

Bomb-sniffing dog

Patron is a Jack Russell Terrier service dog from Ukraine that gained international fame during the Russian invasion of Ukraine. The symbol of the International Coordination Center for Humanitarian Demining formed under the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine.

On May 8, 2022, Volodymyr Zelenskyi awarded Patron with the medal "For Devoted Service" and his owner Mykhailo Ipev with the Order "For Courage" of the III degree.

Gratitude from the Head of the State Emergency Service of Ukraine Serhii Kruk for demining the territory of Chernihiv region.

On May 27, 2022, at the 75th Cannes Film Festival, Patron received the PalmDogManitarian award, an award given for the connection between humanity and dogs.

On June 23, 2022, the dog received the Golden Paw, an award from the Irish Kennel Club.

July 12, 2022, the dog received a certificate and a combat coin from two prestigious US organizations: "Veterans of Foreign Wars[en] and Bomb Techs Without Borders.

On November 20, 2022, Patron became the first dog in history to be awarded the title of "Patron as Goodwill AmbassaDOG in Ukraine" by UNICEF.
